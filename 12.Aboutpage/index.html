Leaders Talk

With over four decades of industry expertise, Kishore is seasoned veteran in the stock broking realm, witnessing the transformative journey from traditional paper trades to the advent of paperless trading. Kishore’s unwavering ability to adapt to cutting-edge technologies has been instrumental in shaping the success of Tradejini.

Driven by an entrepreneurial spirit at the age of 45, Kishore along with his co-founder Dinesh founded Tradejini in 2012, employing a completely bootstrapped approach to create a robust platform that caters to the diverse needs of traders and investors. His uncanny knack of identifying investment opportunities, decoding complex financial data and ability to multi task has been made him a proficient investor and trader!

He was a member of Bangalore Stock Exchange and contributed significantly as the Head of Retail Equities for Way2Wealth Securities, pioneering the launch of the first integrated online trading platform. Beyond the financial sphere, Kishore indulges in the enriching pursuit of travel, exploring the world and gaining inspiration from diverse cultures and landscapes.

Kishore Kumar J, CEO

Leaders Talk

As a finance veteran and with over three decades of interest in the capital markets, Dinesh brings a wealth of experience and an exceptional track record to the table. Throughout his illustrious journey, he has successfully managed multiple businesses, showcasing remarkable entrepreneurial skills.

In 2012, Dinesh co-founded Tradejini alongside Kishore, bootstrapping the company and propelling it to prominence within the industry. His meticulous attention to detail ensures precision and accuracy and ensures we are compliant in all aspects of the work at Tradejini!

Driven by his passion as an avid long-term investor, Dinesh has always nurtured a deep interest in finance and accounts. He has consistently demonstrated resilience and adaptability in the face of evolving compliance regulations with finesse, consistently achieving outstanding results. Beyond his professional pursuits, he enjoys a good game of cricket, humming to old Bollywood music and public speaking.

Dinesh Kumar M, MD
